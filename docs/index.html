The OID Directory


The "OID Directory" is an EXPERIMENTAL Internet Draft (ID) series written by Jesse Coretta.

The documents in the series define terms, procedures and abstract components required for managing the OID spectrum -- in part or in whole -- under the standard X.500/LDAP model.
